rakhelm has reached a choice of two doors, each with its own keeper.

The door keepers explain in unison that one door leads to certain death, while the other leads further into the castle.

To help chose the correct door they recite a riddle...

"The last is in me,
You are the one before,
But trailing behind the first of last,
The second in the whole family has come first."

Can you solve the riddle and choose which keeper is in front of the right door?
